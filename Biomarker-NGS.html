Next Generation Sequencing

List of NGS tests on the pipeline.

We are trying to run amplicon-based library preparation on Illumina iSeq1000, MiniSeq, NextSeq 500 and NextSeq 550.

1. Somatic and Germ line BRCA Testing
The AmpliSeq for Illumina BRCA Panel is a targeted resequencing assay for research of somatic and germline variants across BRCA1 and BRCA2 genes. Targeting all exonic regions and flanking intronic sequences of BRCA1 and BRCA2, and detecting somatic mutations as low as 5% variant allele frequency using cloud based analysis.
Germline mutation is also supplemented by MLPA on peripheral leukocyte DNA.

Consent is necessary for ordering this test. (Engish) (Chinese)
Pamphlets for BRCA testing in high staged, high grade ovarian cancer (English)(Chinese)

2. Focus Panel for Solid Tumor (somatic DNA + RNA)
The AmpliSeq for Illumina Focus Panel is a targeted resequencing assay for biomarker analysis of 52 genes with known relevance to solid tumors. All variants are confirmed by orthogonal methods (PCR-Sanger sequencing, RT-PCR + Sanger, FISH, etc.)

3. Comprehensive Panel for Solild Tumor (somatic DNA + RNA)
The AmpliSeq for Illumina Comprehensive Panel v3 offers coverage of key cancer genes, including kinase domains and genes involved in DNA repair, targetting 161 unique cancer-associated genes
