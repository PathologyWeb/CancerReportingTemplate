Next Generation Sequencing

Next Generation Sequencing is run in the Illumina iSeq100, MiniSeq, NextSeq 500 and NextSeq 550.

The AmpliSeq for Illumina BRCA Panel is a targeted resequencing assay for research of somatic and germline variants across BRCA1 and BRCA2 genes. Targeting all exonic regions and flanking intronic sequences (~20 bp) of the BRCA1 and BRCA2 genes, and detecting somatic mutations down to 5% variant allele frequency using cloud based analysis.
Somatic mutation testing is done on the FFPE block or cytology cell block. Germline mutation testing is performed on peripheral white cells from the EDTA blood sample and is also supplemented by MLPA.

Consent is necessary for ordering this test. (Engish) (Chinese)

Pamphlets for BRCA testing in high staged, high grade ovarian cancer (English)(Chinese)

The AmpliSeq for Illumina Focus Panel is a targeted resequencing assay for biomarker analysis of 52 genes with known relevance to solid tumors. All variants are confirmed by orthogonal methods (PCR-Sanger sequencing, RT-PCR + Sanger, FISH, etc.)

The AmpliSeq for Illumina Comprehensive Panel v3 offers coverage of key cancer genes, including kinase domains and genes involved in DNA repair, targetting 161 unique cancer-associated genes
