Next Generation Sequencing

Next Generation Sequencing is ran on the Illumina MiniSeq and NextSeq 500, using "sequencing by synthesis" chemistry, upon different targeted sequencing panels.

Genomic DNA QC : Quantified by Qubit and the integrity control by conventional PCR
Library QC : Check by automatic electrophoresis (Bioanalyser)
Sequencing QC : Coverage > 500x, Q30 > 80%

Bioinformatics :
DNA Amplicon (BaseSpace Workflow) 2.1.0.19
via Illumina Local Run Manager on a desktop PC [DNA Amplicon Workflow (3.24.1.8+ master), BWA-MEM Whole-Genome(Aligner)0.7.9a-isis-1.0.2, Pisces Variant Caller 5.2.9.23, Illumina Annotation Engine 2.0.11-0- g7fb24a09, Bam Metrics v0.0.22, and SAMtools 0.1.19-isis-1.0.3]


Curation :
Human GRCh37/hg19, BRCA Exchange, ClinVar, VarSome


Limitations : Our system could detect SNV-indel type mutations with a variant allelic frequency greater or equal to 5%. Due to the nature of FFPE specimen and limitations of the amplicon-based library preparation, false negativity, including, but not limited to, large deletion, large duplication and gross chromosomal abnormalities, cannot be completely excluded.

Somatic mutation testing is done on the FFPE block or cytology cell block.

Germline mutation testing is performed on FFPE block and peripheral white cells from the EDTA blood sample, and is also supplemented by MLPA.

Sequencing : The AmpliSeq for Illumina BRCA Panel is a targeted resequencing assay for somatic and germline variants across BRCA1 and BRCA2 genes, targeting all the coding exonic regions and the flanking intronic sequences of the BRCA1 and BRCA2 genes.

MLPA : SALSA MLPA Probemix kits (MRC, Holland): P002 BRCA1, P090 BRCA2 and P239 BRCA1 region;
confirmed by P087 BRCA1 Confirmation or P077 BRCA2 Confirmation whichever is applicable; Coffalyzer.Net software v.210604.1451 (database v.210226.000) for quality control, visualization and analysis of data.

All findings are to be confirmed by orthogonal methods, including Sanger sequencing and others.

Consent is necessary for ordering this test (either somatic test alone, or somatic and germline tests). (Engish) (Chinese)
