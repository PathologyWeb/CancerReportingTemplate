Fluorescence in situ hybridization tests

List of the FISH tests available in the laboratory.
Click the web-link for further information for each of these tests.
Please order the test through the desktop little fox.

  • 1p19q co-deletion (Vysis) For glioma. Please use Ronnie's template for scoring. Reading total 200 tumor nuclei.

  • BCL2 (BCL2 apoptosis regulator) translocation (Vysis) For identification of double hit or triple hit high-grade B-cell lymphomas.

  • BCL6 (BCL6 transcription repressor) translocation (Vysis) For identification of double hit or triple hit high-grade B-cell lymphomas.

  • BCOR (BCL6 corepressor) translocation (Empire Genomics and Propriety, China) For BRCOR gene rearrange in Ewing-like blue round cell tumor.

  • BRAF (B-Raf proto-oncogene, serine/threonine kinase) amplification (Zytolight) BRAF/CEN7 amplification probe, esp KIAA-BRAF translocation in e.g. pilocystic astrocytoma.

  • BRAF (B-Raf proto-oncogene, serine/threonine kinase) translocation (Zytolight) For BRAF translocations in melanocytic nevi, pilocytic astrocytomas, malignant melanoma, prostate and gastric cancer. BRAF/CEN7 amplification probe in evaluation (esp KIAA-BRAF translocation).

  • CDNK2A (cyclin dependent kinase inhibitor 2A) deletion (Zytolight) For glioma, looking for CDNK2A/CEN9 for detecting deletion.

  • CIC (capicua transcriptional repressor) translocation (Zytolight) For EWSR1-negative small blue round cell tumors.

  • CSF1 (colony stimulating factor 1) translocation (Propriety, China) For giant cell tumor of tendon shealth and pigmented villonodular synovitis.

  • CSF1R (colony stimulating factor 1 receptor) translocation (Zytolight) Acute megakaryoblastic leukemia cell line generating the RBM6-CSF1R fusion gene. This probe is probably not useful in AP.

  • DDIT3 (DNA damage inducible transcript 3) translocation (Vysis and (Zytolight) For myxoid liposarcoma.

  • EGFR (epidermal growth factor receptor) gene amplification (Zytolight) For EGFR amplification. EGFR/CEN7 dual color probe.

  • EWSR1 (EWS RNA binding protein 1) translocation (Zytolight) See Sanger's Catalogue of Somatic Mutations In Cancer (COSMIC) fusion database for potential partners of EWSR1.

  • FUS (FUS RNA binding protein) translocation (Vysis) For myxoid liposarcoma.

  • JAZF1 (JAZF zinc finger 1) translocation (Zytovision) About 50% low grade endometrial stromal sarcoma.

  • HER2 (erb-b2 receptor tyrosine kinase 2) gene amplification (Vysis) For breast cancers. Please order DISH for more efficient processing.

  • MAML2 (mastermind like transcriptional coactivator 2) translocation (Zytolight) For mucoepidermoid carcinoma.

  • MDM2 (Murine Double Minute 2) gene amplification (Vysis and Zytolight) Distinguish well differentiated liposarcoma from benign adipose tumors; distinguish dedifferentiated liposarcoma from poorly differentiated sarcomas; distinguish low grade osteosarcoma (positive) from benign fibrous and fibro-osseous lesions (negative, Mod Pathol 2011;24:624).

  • MET (MET proto-oncogene, receptor tyrosine kinase) gene amplification (Zytolight) For the detection of MET gene amplifications found in a variety of human tumors including lung, breast, colorectal, prostate, and gastric carcinomas as well as in gliomas, melanomas and some sarcomas.

  • MYB (MYB proto-oncogene, transcription factor) translocation (Zytolight) For adenoid cystic carcinoma, also consider NFIB and MYBL1 break apart FISH tests.

  • MYBL1 (MYB Proto-oncogene Like 1) translocation (Empire Genomics) For adenoid cystic carcinoma, also consider MYB and NFIB break apart FISH tests.

  • c-Myc (MYC proto-oncogene, bHLH transcription factor) translocation (Vysis) In high-grade B-cell lymphomas, the presence of MYC aberrations identifies a patient subset with a very poor prognosis, particularly when there is concomitant rearrangement of BCL2 or BCL6, a condition referred to as "double hit” DLBCL. In rare cases translocation involves MYC, BCL2 and BCL6, so called “triple hit”. MYC translocations confer a worse prognosis in patients treated with cyclophosphamide, doxorubicin, vincristine, and prednisone (CHOP), both in combination with and without rituximab.

  • N-myc (MYCN proto-oncogene, bHLH transcription factor) amplification (Vysis) For neuroblastoma.

  • NFIB (Nuclear Factor I B) translocation (Empire Genomics) For adenoid cystic carcinoma, also consider MYB and MYBL1 break apart FISH tests.

  • NR4A3 (nuclear receptor subfamily 4 group A member 3) translocation (Zytolight) For extraskeletal myxoid chondrosarcoma, among differential diagnosis of EWSR1 break apart-positive tumors.

  • NTRK 1, 2 & 3 (neurotrophic receptor tyrosine kinase 1, 2 & 3) translocations (Zytolight) To detect translocations involving the chromosomal regions 1q23.1 harboring the NTRK1 gene, 9q21.33 harboring the NTRK2 gene, and 15q25.3 harboring the NTRK3 gene, respectively.

  • PHF1 (PHD finger protein 1) translocation (Zytolight) For Endometrial stromal tumors (ESTs).

  • PTEN (phosphatase and tensin homolog) deletion probe (Zytolight) PTEN/CEN10 dual color probe for deletion.

  • RET (RET proto-oncogene) translocation (Zytolight) To detect translocations involving the chromosomal region 10q11.21 harboring the RET gene observed e.g. in lung adenocarcinoma. Cut off as low as 15% tumor nuclei.

  • ROS1 (ROS proto-oncogene 1, receptor tyrosine kinase) translocation (Vysis and Zytovision) For confirmation of ROS1 translocation after IHC test (Zytovison and Vysis probes are available).

  • SS18 (SS18 subunit of BAF chromatin remodeling complex) translocation (Zytolight) For synovial sarcoma.

  • TFE3 (transcription factor binding to IGHM enhancer 3) translocation (Zytolight) For renal cell carcinomas (RCCs) which usually affect children and adolescents, and alveolar soft part sarcoma.

  • USP6 (ubiquitin specific peptidase 6) translocation (Zytolight) For nodular fasciitis and primary aneurysmal bone cyst.

  • YWHAE translocation (Zytolight) For high grade endometrial stromal sarcoma and clear cell sarcoma of kidney.
