Fluorescence in situ hybridization tests

Lising of the FISH tests available in the laboratory.
Click the web-link for further information for each of these tests.
Please order the test through the desktop little fox.

  • 1p19q co-deletion probes For glioma.

  • BCL2 (BCL2 apoptosis regulator) dual color breakapart probe For identification of double hit or triple hit high-grade B-cell lymphomas.

  • BCL6 (BCL6 transcription repressor) dual color breakapart probe For identification of double hit or triple hit high-grade B-cell lymphomas.

  • CIC (capicua transcriptional repressor) dual color breakapart probe For EWSR1-negative small blue round cell tumors.

  • CSF1 (colony stimulating factor 1) dual color breakapart probe For pigmented villonodular synovitis.

  • DDIT3 (DNA damage inducible transcript 3) dual color breakapart probe For myxoid liposarcoma.

  • EGFR (epidermal growth factor receptor) gene amplification probe Not for the detection of common EGFR mutations. Not recommended for use in non small cell lung cancers according to 2018 CAP-IASLC-AMP guidelines.

  • EWSR1 (EWS RNA binding protein 1) dual color breakapart probe See Sanger's Catalogue of Somatic Mutations In Cancer (COSMIC) fusion database for potential partners of EWSR1.

  • FUS (FUS RNA binding protein) dual color breakapart probe For myxoid liposarcoma.

  • HER2 (erb-b2 receptor tyrosine kinase 2) gene amplification probe For breast cancers.

  • MAML2 (mastermind like transcriptional coactivator 2) dual color breakapart probe For mucoepidermoid carcinoma.

  • c-Myc (MYC proto-oncogene, bHLH transcription factor) dual color breakapart probe In high-grade B-cell lymphomas, the presence of MYC aberrations identifies a patient subset with a very poor prognosis, particularly when there is concomitant rearrangement of BCL2 or BCL6, a condition referred to as "double hit” DLBCL. In rare cases translocation involves MYC, BCL2 and BCL6, so called “triple hit”. MYC translocations confer a worse prognosis in patients treated with cyclophosphamide, doxorubicin, vincristine, and prednisone (CHOP), both in combination with and without rituximab.

  • N-myc (MYCN proto-oncogene, bHLH transcription factor) probe For neuroblastoma.

  • NR4A3 (nuclear receptor subfamily 4 group A member 3) dual color breakapart probe For extraskeletal myxoid chondrosarcoma, among differential diagnosis of EWSR1 break apart-positive tumors.

  • MDM2 (Murine Double Minute 2) gene amplification probe Distinguish well differentiated liposarcoma from benign adipose tumors; distinguish dedifferentiated liposarcoma from poorly differentiated sarcomas; distinguish low grade osteosarcoma (positive) from benign fibrous and fibro-osseous lesions (negative, Mod Pathol 2011;24:624)

  • ROS1 (ROS proto-oncogene 1, receptor tyrosine kinase) dual color breakapart probe For confirmation of ROS1 translocation after IHC test.

  • SS18 (SS18 subunit of BAF chromatin remodeling complex) dual color breakapart probe For synovial sarcoma.
